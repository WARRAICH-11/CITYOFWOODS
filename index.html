Our Story

Founded in 1985, City of Woods has been at the forefront of furniture design and craftsmanship. What began as a small workshop has grown into a globally recognized brand, known for our commitment to quality, sustainability, and innovative design.

Our team of skilled artisans combines traditional techniques with modern technology to create pieces that are not only beautiful but built to last for generations.
